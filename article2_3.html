Forum Nová Karolina

From the view of Louison
  • Louison
  • March 27, 2023

The Forum Nová Karolina is a shopping and entertainment center located in Ostrava, Czech Republic. Opened in 2012, this modern complex symbolizes the urban and economic revitalization of Ostrava. As a former declining industrial city, Ostrava has reinvented itself through projects such as this one. The Forum Nová Karolina illustrates the city's transformation and its desire to become a top destination for shopping, entertainment, and tourism in Central Europe. The center is located on the historic site of a former marshalling yard that was abandoned in the 1990s. The redevelopment project was designed by French architect Jean-Paul Viguier, who successfully integrated the industrial remains of the past into a modern and bold architecture. The center is easily accessible by public transportation, thanks in part to the central station in Ostrava which is located just a few minutes' walk away. With an area of 58,000 square meters, the Forum Nová Karolina offers a unique shopping experience with over 200 shops and boutiques, including international brands, local brands, and designer shops. Visitors can enjoy a wide selection of clothing, shoes, accessories, electronics, toys, and sports equipment, to name a few. The center also features a supermarket and various specialized stores, such as pharmacies, opticians, and banks. In addition to its commercial offerings, the Forum Nová Karolina offers a multitude of dining options to satisfy all tastes and desires. Visitors can choose from a wide range of restaurants, cafes, and fast food outlets that offer cuisines from around the world. The center also has a modern and welcoming dining area, where everyone can enjoy a variety of dishes in a pleasant and lively setting. The Forum Nová Karolina is also a place of entertainment for families and visitors of all ages. The center has an ultramodern multiplex cinema with several screening rooms, showing the latest international and Czech releases. Children can enjoy an indoor play area, while adults can relax in one of the many relaxation and leisure areas, or participate in cultural and artistic events regularly organized by the center. One of the most notable aspects of the Forum Nová Karolina is its commitment to sustainable development and the environment. The center was designed with ecological materials and eco-energy technologies, such as LED lighting and energy recovery systems. In addition, it offers sustainable mobility solutions, such as electric vehicle charging stations and bicycle parking spaces.

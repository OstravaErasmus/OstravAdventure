Ostrava zoo

From the point of view of Dorian
  • Dorian
  • March 6, 2023

The Ostrava Zoo, located in the city of Ostrava in the Czech Republic, is one of the largest and most popular zoos in the country. Spread over an area of 110 hectares, the zoo is home to over 4000 animals from around the world, making it a popular destination for tourists and locals alike.

Ice hockey is one of the most popular sports in the Czech Republic, and the city of Ostrava is no exception. Home to two professional hockey teams, HC Oceláři Ostrava and HC Verva Litvínov, the city has a rich history and culture of ice hockey.

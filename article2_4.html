My Budapest Trip

From the view of Louison
  • Louison
  • April 17, 2023

We went for a weekend with friends to Budapest. I will tell about our trip and the places we have all visited Together.

After visiting the great park of Budapest we went to Heroes Square, it is one of the central places of the city of Budapest. This place celebrates the Hungarian heroes, in particular the heroes of the 1st World War but also the Hungarian warriors. There were a lot of people there. She is very popular in Hungary.

Heroes 'Square


Budapest Parliament


Along the Danube we passed in front of the Hungarian parliament, it is an important place and we could not get closer to it because it was locked by barriers and the police.

Budavàr Palace


Always along the Danube we could see Budapest Palace in the distance, despite the 2 kilometers that separated us from it, it still seemed immense. It is one of the largest palaces in Europe and dominates the right bank of the Danube.

Since we were in Hungary we wanted to taste the typical dishes from here, so we all had a goulash soup at the restaurant. It's a kind of stew with tomato sauce.

Goulash soup


I also ate typical Hungarian pasta, Galuska accompanied by beef.

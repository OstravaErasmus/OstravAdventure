Hangover

in Budapest

  • Dorian
  • April 16, 2023

The Morrison is a unique bar located in the heart of Budapest that offers a one-of-a-kind experience for anyone looking to enjoy a night out in the city. During my recent visit to Budapest, I had the chance to visit this famous establishment and I must say, I was thoroughly impressed with what I saw. One of the standout features of the Morrison is the outdoor courtyard located in the center of the bar. It serves as a central gathering spot for patrons to relax and socialize, while enjoying a cold drink on a warm summer night. The courtyard is beautifully decorated with a variety of plants and foliage, creating a relaxing atmosphere that is perfect for winding down after a long day of sightseeing. Inside the bar, there are three separate rooms, each with a different ambiance and atmosphere. One room features three billiard tables, but unfortunately, we were unable to play a game due to the crowded conditions. Another room features a karaoke setup, which was a popular attraction for many of the patrons, though we decided not to subject the Hungarian locals to our singing skills. The main attraction of the Morrison, however, is the unlimited drink option. For a flat fee of 16 euros, patrons can enjoy unlimited drinks from 6 pm until 5 am. This includes a wide range of beer, wine, and spirits, making it an excellent value for anyone looking to have a good time without breaking the bank. Despite the lively atmosphere and the open bar, the Morrison manages to maintain a relaxed and friendly vibe. The staff are warm and welcoming, creating an inviting atmosphere that is perfect for making new friends and enjoying a night out on the town. Overall, the Morrison is a must-visit destination for anyone looking to experience the nightlife of Budapest. With its unique outdoor courtyard, multiple rooms, and unlimited drink option, it offers a truly memorable experience that is sure to leave a lasting impression. So, grab some friends, head to the Morrison, and get ready for a night you won't soon forget!

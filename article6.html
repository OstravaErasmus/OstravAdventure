Street art

in Czech-Republic
  • Dorian
  • Feb 13, 2023


Ostrava is a city that is quickly gaining a reputation for its thriving street art scene. During my recent visit to the city, I was amazed by the diverse range of street art that could be found throughout the city, from small and subtle pieces tucked away in hidden corners to large and bold murals that dominate entire buildings. What struck me most about the street art in Ostrava was its sheer creativity and originality. Many of the works were atypical and surprising, not only in terms of their size and placement, but also in their use of unconventional materials and techniques. One particular work that caught my attention was a mural located near the city center that depicted the face of a woman gazing up towards the sky. The piece was stunning, with its intricate details drawing the viewer in and inviting them to contemplate its meaning. Despite being in black and white, the mural was still captivating, and a testament to the skill and creativity of the artist who created it. It was a reminder of the power that street art can have in transforming ordinary public spaces into something truly extraordinary. Another work that stood out to me was a large mural that covered the entire side of a building in the city's industrial district. The mural featured a series of abstract shapes and patterns that seemed to shift and change depending on the angle from which it was viewed. It was an impressive and thought-provoking piece, one that spoke to the city's industrial heritage while also challenging traditional notions of what street art can be. Throughout the city, there were countless other examples of street art that were equally impressive and inspiring. From colorful murals to playful installations, each work was a testament to the creativity and ingenuity of the artists who created them. In conclusion, Ostrava's street art scene is a true gem that deserves to be explored and celebrated. The works are atypical, surprising, and often breathtaking, reflecting the city's rich history and culture. Whether you're a fan of street art or simply looking to explore a new side of the city, a visit to Ostrava's street art scene is sure to be an unforgettable experience.
